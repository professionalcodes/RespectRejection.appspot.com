What is RespectRejection.appspot.com?

Respect Rejection is a site that allows you to purchase a phone number and customize your own rejection hotline. You maintain ownership of the number and when that horrible ugly person asks you for your number, you give them your own personal rejection hotline number that you made. Inspiration for this site came from my experience of being at bars and restaurants and seeing what waitresses and female bartenders have to deal with. That being said this site is just as equally made for men as it is women, but let's face it. Women have more to deal with.
